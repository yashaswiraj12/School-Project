OUR STORY

Dream India Schools (DIS), an initiative to provide quality English medium technology-led education, conquering the twin challenges of Accessibility & Affordability. Many parents, due to constraints of Accessibility and Affordability.

fail to provide their children the much deserved quality education.

DIS thus makes the pedestal for every such parent in fulfilling their most important part of parenting responsibility. By establishing schools in small towns and semi-urban areas, DIS has become a trail blazing pioneer of quality education. What’s next? The more and the best is yet to come!

VISION


MISSION

Our mission is to make our school a caring and welcoming place where every student can do their best in studies, make friends, and feel good about themselves.

We want to help kids love learning by teaching them not just academic stuff, but also how to think, be creative, and understand their own culture. Besides classrooms, we also want to teach values like respect and responsibility.

Our lessons will include the rich traditions of India and also show how we're all part of a big world. We want our students to grow up as kind and smart people who can make the world a better place.
